Welcome to Jinxwords. In this game, you are given 9 letters. Using only these letters, find as many words as you can in the time you have. Words must be at least 3 letters long, and you may only use as many copies of a single letter as you are given. The complete word list is the SOWPODS corpus.

Select a game mode using the interface above.

In multiplayer mode, the player hosting the room chooses the game type, moderates the player list, and triggers the start of games.

All players choose a username of up to 15 alphanumeric characters. Hosts create a room by choosing a unique room handle. Guests join a room by submitting its handle.

Multiplayer games

Uniques is similar to single-player mode. You have 3 minutes to find as many words as possible which are different to those of your co-players.

Jinx is turn-based sudden death. Each round, you have 15 seconds to find and choose a word which none of your co-players have chosen. Words must not have already been played. If you cannot find a word within the time limit, you will be eliminated from further play.

In both game types, the player with the highest score at the end will be the winner.
